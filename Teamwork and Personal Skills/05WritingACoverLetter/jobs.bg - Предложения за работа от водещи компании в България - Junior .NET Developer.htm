Junior .NET Developer
Ref.No: DEVN2
Описание и
Изисквания:		 We are an IT services company providing consulting, software development and outsourcing services. Our vision is to have an efficient, steadily growing business empowered by the right technology. We are focused on finding the best customer solution, instead of selecting a predefined product offering. Our mission is to attract and develop talent, as it is the most valuable asset.

We are looking for a junior application developer with the following profile:

Role and Responsibilities:
• Design and develop application modules according to the specified requirements
• Analyze and troubleshoot issues in the software
• Self organized and motivated to work in a team
• Develop and execute unit tests as part of the continuous integration process
• Provide application support and maintenance

Requirements and Qualifications:
• Solid knowledge in OOP, algorithms and data structures
• Application development skills and knowledge of C#/.NET
• Experience in web development - HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery
• Database modelling and SQL skills
• Graduate or student in IT technology related field
• Willingness to learn and apply new technologies individually and in a team
• Fluency in English

We offer:
• Open working environment where we work and have fun
• Opportunity to learn from top experts and grow professionally
• Very competitive compensation package depending on personal achievements
• Challenging projects where you can fulfill your potential
• Regular company sponsored trainings and certifications

If you are interested, please send us your CV in English. Only short-listed applicants will be contacted.
All applications will be treated with strict confidentiality.
